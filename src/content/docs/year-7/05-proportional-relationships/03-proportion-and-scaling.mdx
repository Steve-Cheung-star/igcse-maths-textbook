## 1. Inquiry: Constant Relationships
**Inquiry Question:** *If 3 pens cost 6 dollars, how much would 10 pens cost, and why does the price per pen stay the same?*

Proportion describes a situation where two ratios are equal. If you double the amount of one thing (like ingredients in a cake), you must double the other thing to keep the relationship the same.



---

## 2. The Unitary Method
The easiest way to solve proportion problems is to find the value of **one unit** first.

**Steps:**
1.  **Find the unit value:** Divide the total by the number of items you know.
2.  **Scale up:** Multiply that unit value by the number of items you want to find.

**Example: If 5 apples cost 2.50 dollars, how much do 8 apples cost?**
1.  Find the cost of 1 apple: $2.50 \div 5 = 0.50$ dollars.
2.  Find the cost of 8 apples: $0.50 \times 8 = 4.00$ dollars.

---

## 3. Best Buys
We use proportion to decide which product offers better value for money by calculating the "Unit Price."

**Example:**
* Shop A: 2 liters of milk for 3.40 dollars ($1.70 per liter)
* Shop B: 3 liters of milk for 4.50 dollars ($1.50 per liter)
* **Shop B is the best buy.**

## 4. Practice & Homework

### Q1: Recipe Scaling
<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="📝 Question">
    A recipe for 4 people uses 200g of flour and 2 eggs. How much of each ingredient is needed for:
    
    a) 8 people?
    
    b) 10 people?
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="✅ Solution">
    a) 8 is double 4. Flour: 400g, Eggs: 4.
    
    b) Find for 1 person: 50g flour, 0.5 eggs. 
    Multiply by 10: **500g flour and 5 eggs**.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Q2: Unitary Method Practice
<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="📝 Question">
    Solve the following:
    
    a) If 6 towels cost 42 dollars, how much do 5 towels cost?
    
    b) If a car travels 150 km on 15 liters of fuel, how far can it travel on 40 liters?
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="✅ Solution">
    a) 1 towel = 7 dollars. 5 towels = **35 dollars**.
    
    b) 1 liter = 10 km. 40 liters = **400 km**.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Q3: Best Buy Challenge
<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="📝 Question">
    Which is the better value?
    
    Pack A: 500g of pasta for 1.20 dollars
    Pack B: 750g of pasta for 1.65 dollars
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="✅ Solution">
    Pack A: 1.20 / 5 = 0.24 dollars per 100g.
    Pack B: 1.65 / 7.5 = 0.22 dollars per 100g.
    
    **Pack B** is the better value.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Q4: Currency Conversion
<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="📝 Question">
    The exchange rate is 1 US Dollar = 0.90 Euros.
    
    a) How many Euros would you get for 200 Dollars?
    
    b) How many Dollars would you get for 45 Euros?
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="✅ Solution">
    a) $200 \times 0.90 = 180 \text{ Euros}$.
    
    b) $45 \div 0.90 = 50 \text{ Dollars}$.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
