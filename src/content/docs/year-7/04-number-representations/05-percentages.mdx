## 1. Inquiry: The Century Scale
**Inquiry Questions:** *What does "percent" actually mean in Latin? Why is 100 the most common denominator in the real world?*

The word **percent** comes from "per centum," meaning "for every hundred." Percentages are just fractions with a denominator of 100. They allow us to compare different totals on a standardized scale.

### Visualizing Percentages

* **50%** is $50/100$, which simplifies to **1/2**.
* **25%** is $25/100$, which simplifies to **1/4**.
* **10%** is $10/100$, which simplifies to **1/10**.

---

## 2. Calculating Percentages of Amounts
There are two main strategies for finding a percentage of a number:

### Strategy A: The "Unit" Method (Mental Math)
1. Find **10%** by dividing the amount by 10.
2. Find **1%** by dividing the amount by 100.
3. Combine these to find other values.
   * *Example: Find 15% of 200.*
   * 10% is 20.
   * 5% is half of that (10).
   * 15% = 20 + 10 = **30**.

### Strategy B: The Multiplier Method (Calculator)
Convert the percentage to a decimal and multiply.
* *Example: Find 17% of 80.*
* $0.17 \times 80 = 13.6$
