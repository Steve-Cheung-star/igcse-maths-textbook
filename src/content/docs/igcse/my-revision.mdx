    Take full control of your exam prep with these built-in tools:

    * **⭐ Bookmark the Best:** Stuck on a tricky algebra problem? Click the star icon to bookmark it. Use the **Bookmarks** filter to quickly review your most challenging questions before exam day.
    * **🔍 Smart Search:** Looking for that one specific geometry question? Use the search bar to instantly filter your history by topic, difficulty level, or specific keywords inside the question.
    * **🧠 Import External AI:** Did you use the **"Copy Prompt"** button from one of our chapters to generate a question in Google Gemini? Click "Import External AI" and paste the exact response here. This privately saves the question to your local history.
    * **📡 Live Revision Portal:** Study with a classmate! Enter a room name and hit Connect (or hit **✨ Magic** to instantly create a random one). When your friends share questions, they will pop up here with an **📥 Import** button to add to your own history.
    * **📋 Copy & Broadcast:** Click the clipboard icon to copy the text of any question for your own digital notes. **Pro-Tip:** If you are connected to a Revision Portal room, clicking copy will *instantly broadcast* that specific question to everyone else in the room!

    > **🔒 Privacy Note:** Your revision history is entirely private and stored locally on your current device. *If you switch computers, use a private/incognito window, or clear your browser data, this history will reset (unless you beam it to another device via the Revision Portal!)*
