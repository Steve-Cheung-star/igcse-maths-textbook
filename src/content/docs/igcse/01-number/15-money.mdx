## 1. Calculating with Money

When performing basic arithmetic with money, the most important rule is **formatting**. 

* **Two Decimal Places:** Most global currencies are subdivided into 100 parts (pence, cents). Therefore, answers must *always* be given to exactly two decimal places, even if the second decimal is a zero. 
  * *Correct:* 4.50 GBP
  * *Incorrect:* 4.5 GBP
* **Do Not Round Early:** If a calculation involves multiple steps, keep the full decimal on your calculator and only round the *final* answer to two decimal places.

## 2. Currency Conversion (The Exchange Rate)

An exchange rate tells you how much one currency is worth in another currency. While these are often given as a single unit (e.g., 1 GBP = 1.25 USD), they can also be presented as non-unit ratios (e.g., 5 EUR = 6 USD). 

The traditional way to approach conversions is to memorise a multiply/divide rule, but this *only* works easily if one side of the exchange rate is a "1" (the base currency).

## 3. The Ratio Method (The Foolproof Strategy)

By setting up a **ratio table**, you don't need to memorize rules, and it works perfectly even when neither currency is a "1".

**Example:** The exchange rate is 5 USD = 4 GBP. Convert 450 USD into GBP.

1. **Set up the headings:** Write the two currencies as a ratio.
   **USD : GBP**
2. **Write the exchange rate:** **5 : 4**
3. **Write the value you know:** Place the 450 under the USD column.
   **USD : GBP**
   **5 : 4**
   **450 : ?**
4. **Find the scale factor:** How do you get from 5 to 450? You divide! **450 ÷ 5 = 90**. 
5. **Apply it to the other side:** Multiply the 4 by 90. The answer is **360 GBP**.

</SteveTip>

## 4. Standard Practice Problems

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="📝 Question 1: Money Calculations">
    * **a)** A customer buys 3 coffees at 2.85 GBP each and 2 pastries at 1.50 GBP each. They pay with a 20.00 GBP note. Calculate their change.
    * **b)** A RuneScape monthly membership costs 12.49 USD per month. How much does it cost for a full year of membership?
    * **c)** A pack of 5 identical cinema tickets costs 62.50 AUD. What is the cost of exactly 1 ticket?
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="✅ Solution 1">
    * **a)** Cost of coffees: 3 × 2.85 = 8.55 GBP. Cost of pastries: 2 × 1.50 = 3.00 GBP. Total bill: 11.55 GBP. Change: 20.00 - 11.55 = 8.45. **Answer: 8.45 GBP**
    * **b)** Multiply monthly membership cost by 12: 12.49 × 12 = 149.88. **Answer: 149.88 USD**
    * **c)** Divide total cost by 5: 62.50 ÷ 5 = 12.5. Remember to always format money to two decimal places! **Answer: 12.50 AUD**
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

    * **a)** A traveler exchanges 600 GBP and receives 750 USD. Calculate the exchange rate in the form 4 GBP = x USD.
    * **b)** A student exchanges 400 EUR and receives 4500 NOK (Norwegian Krone). Calculate the exchange rate in the form 8 EUR = x NOK.

  <TabItem label="✅ Solution 4">

    * **b)** Ratio EUR : NOK is 400 : 4500. To scale 400 down to 8, divide by 50. Apply to NOK: 4500 ÷ 50 = 90. **Answer: 8 EUR = 90.00 NOK**
    * **c)** Ratio AUD : NZD is 250 : 270. To scale 250 down to 25, divide by 10. Apply to NZD: 270 ÷ 10 = 27. **Answer: 25 AUD = 27.00 NZD**
  </TabItem>
