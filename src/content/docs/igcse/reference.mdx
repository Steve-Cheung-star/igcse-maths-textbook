## Number and Finance

**Percentage Change:**

$$ \dfrac{\text{New} - \text{Old}}{\text{Old}} \times 100 $$

**Percentage Increase:**

$$ \text{New} = \text{Old} \times \left(1 + \dfrac{r}{100}\right) $$

**Percentage Decrease:**

$$ \text{New} = \text{Old} \times \left(1 - \dfrac{r}{100}\right) $$

**Simple Interest (Interest Only):**

$$ I = \dfrac{P \times r \times n}{100} $$

**Simple Interest (Total Amount):**

$$ A = P(1 + \dfrac{rn}{100}) $$

**Compound Interest:**

$$ A = P\left(1 + \dfrac{r}{100}\right)^n $$

**Speed, Distance, Time:**

$$ s = \dfrac{d}{t} $$

## Algebra and Indices

**Laws of Indices (Multiplication & Division):**

$$ x^a \times x^b = x^{a+b} $$

$$ \dfrac{x^a}{x^b} = x^{a-b} $$

**Laws of Indices (Powers & Zero):**

$$ (x^a)^b = x^{ab} $$

$$ x^0 = 1 $$

**Negative Indices:**

$$ x^{-a} = \dfrac{1}{x^a} $$

$$ \left(\dfrac{a}{b}\right)^{-n} = \left(\dfrac{b}{a}\right)^n $$

**Fractional Indices (Roots):**

$$ x^{\frac{1}{n}} = \sqrt[n]{x} $$

$$ x^{\frac{m}{n}} = (\sqrt[n]{x})^m $$

**Quadratic Formula:**

$$ x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} $$

## Circle Theorems

**Angle at the Centre:**  
The angle at the centre is twice the angle at the circumference. &nbsp;

**Angle in a Semicircle:**  
The angle subtended by a diameter at the circumference is exactly **90°**.

**Angles in the Same Segment:**  
Angles subtended by the same arc at the circumference are equal.

**Cyclic Quadrilateral:**  
Opposite angles in a cyclic quadrilateral sum to **180°**.

**Tangent and Radius:**  
A tangent is perpendicular (**90°**) to the radius at the point of contact.

**Tangents from an External Point:**  
Two tangents drawn from the same external point to a centre are equal in length.

**Alternate Segment Theorem:**  
The angle between a tangent and a chord is equal to the angle in the alternate segment.

**Equal Chords:**  
Equal chords are equidistant from the centre of the circle.

**Perpendicular Bisector of a Chord:**  
The perpendicular bisector of a chord passes through the centre of the circle.

**Radius and Chord:**  
A radius that is perpendicular to a chord bisects the chord.

## Coordinate Geometry

**Distance Between Two Points:**

$$ \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} $$

**Midpoint:**

$$ M = \left( \dfrac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \dfrac{y_1 + y_2}{2} \right) $$

**Gradient (Slope):**

$$ m = \dfrac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} $$

**Parallel Lines:**

$$ m_1 = m_2 $$

**Perpendicular Lines:**

$$ m_2 = -\dfrac{1}{m_1} $$

## Trigonometry

**Pythagoras' Theorem:**

$$ a^2 + b^2 = c^2 $$

**Right-Angled Trigonometry (SOH CAH TOA):**

$$ \sin \theta = \dfrac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Hypotenuse}} $$

$$ \cos \theta = \dfrac{\text{Adjacent}}{\text{Hypotenuse}} $$

$$ \tan \theta = \dfrac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Adjacent}} $$

**Sine Rule:**

$$ \dfrac{a}{\sin A} = \dfrac{b}{\sin B} = \dfrac{c}{\sin C} $$

**Cosine Rule (Finding a Side):**

$$ a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc \cos A $$

**Cosine Rule (Finding an Angle):**

$$ \cos A = \dfrac{b^2 + c^2 - a^2}{2bc} $$

$$ A = \cos^{-1}\left(\dfrac{b^2 + c^2 - a^2}{2bc}\right) $$

**Area of a Triangle (Trigonometric):**

$$ \text{Area} = \dfrac{1}{2}ab \sin C $$

## Probability

**Theoretical Probability:**

$$ P(A) = \dfrac{n(A)}{n(U)} $$

**Expected Frequency:**

$$ \text{Expected} = P(A) \times N $$

**Mutually Exclusive (OR rule):**

$$ P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) $$

**Independent Events (AND rule):**

$$ P(A \cap B) = P(A) \times P(B) $$

**Complementary Events (NOT rule):**

$$ P(\text{Not } A) = 1 - P(A) $$
